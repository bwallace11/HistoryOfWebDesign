Major Contributors to JavaScript

Brendan Eich

Created JavaScript in 1995 at Netscape. Designed the prototype-based object model that remains core to the language.

Allen Wirfs-Brock

Lead editor of ECMAScript 5 and central figure in stabilizing the language after the collapse of ES4.

Ryan Dahl

Creator of Node.js in 2009, enabling server-side JavaScript.

TC39 Committee

Technical committee of Ecma International responsible for evolving the ECMAScript specification through staged proposals.
